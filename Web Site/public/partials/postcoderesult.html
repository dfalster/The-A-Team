What can you do to make sure your child has the best start to life?

There are three main drivers that have a significant affect on your childs development

Quit Smoking

Smoking while pregnant or around a child plays a significant role in their early development
For help on how to quit please visit Quit SA or phone 13 78 48.

Access to transport

You don't have to feel isolated or stuck. If you don't have access to transport you may be eligible for the Child and Family Health Family Home Visiting service.
Please see Family Home Visiting page for more detail.

Ability to afford medicine

If you have to put off purchasing medicine because you cannot afford it, you may be eligible for a health care card. Please see the Department of Human Services page on Concession and health care cards

Child and Family Health Services

The Child and Family Health Service centres promote the health, well-being and development of children, young people and families across South Australia by providing:
  • support to parents in areas of parenting
  • health services for infants, children and young people
  • support for families and children with additional needs
  • up-to-date health information for parents, children and young people

For more information please visit one of the nearby CaFHS centres or go to http://www.cyh.com
